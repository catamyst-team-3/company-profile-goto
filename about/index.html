Company Overview

The gogreen group is the biggest eco-friendly and sustainable furniture manufacturer in Indonesia, with a goal to "empower green material" by offering furniture made of natural materials and a variety of furniture solutions that cater to all tastes and prosper in a sustainable economy. On-demand sustainable materials, custom furniture, furniture delivery, recycling with us, and give-and-take services via the gogreen platforms make up the ecosystem of gogreen.
